About Me

Lindokuhle Skosana is an award-winning South African software engineer and AI specialist, recognized internationally for his pioneering work in fraud detection systems and educational technology innovation.

As the founder of LegitCheck SA, he has developed cutting-edge AI systems that analyze over 50,000 transactions monthly, reducing fraudulent activities by 62% for partner financial institutions across Africa.

Co-founder of Elevate Up, Lindokuhle is at the forefront of transforming South African education through adaptive learning technologies and flexible scheduling platforms that have reached over 10,000 students.
